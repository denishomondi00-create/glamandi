# Offline Operations SOP

Allowed offline: view cached dashboard, tenants, landlords, properties, units, record manual payment drafts, log repairs, capture inquiries, add tenant notes, communication exceptions, utility charge drafts, penalty waiver requests, and landlord repair expense drafts.

Not allowed offline: official receipt generation, payment posting, Paystack, Daraja STK, final statements, payouts, final audit commits, PDF generation, and webhook processing.
